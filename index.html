About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Wanli Ouyang. I obtained both my Ph.D. degree (2023) in University of Sydney and Bachelor degree (2016) in Fudan University (FDU). My Ph.D. advisors are Prof. Wanli Ouyang and Dr. Dong Yuan.

My research interests include human-centric visual computing and human-centric foundation models.
